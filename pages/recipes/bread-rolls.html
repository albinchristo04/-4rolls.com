Homemade Dinner Rolls

Fluffy, golden dinner rolls perfect for any meal

Introduction

These soft, fluffy dinner rolls are the perfect accompaniment to any meal. With a golden crust and tender interior, they're easier to make than you might think. Once you try these homemade rolls, you'll never buy store-bought again.

Ingredients

  • 2¬º teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1¬º cups warm water (110¬∞F)
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3¬Ω cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg yolk mixed with 1 tablespoon water (for brushing)

Instructions

Step 1: Activate Yeast

Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand for 5-10 minutes until foamy.

Step 2: Mix Dough

Add oil and salt to yeast mixture. Gradually add flour until a soft dough forms. Knead 8-10 minutes until smooth.

Step 3: First Rise

Place in greased bowl, cover, and rise 1 hour until doubled.

Step 4: Shape Rolls

Punch down dough and divide into 16 pieces. Shape each into a ball and place in greased muffin tin.

Step 5: Second Rise

Cover and rise 30 minutes until puffy.

Step 6: Bake

Preheat oven to 375¬∞F. Brush with egg wash and bake 15-20 minutes until golden brown.

Tips for Success

  • Room temperature ingredients help the dough rise better
  • Don't over-flour the dough - it should be soft and slightly sticky
  • Brush with melted butter immediately after baking for extra softness

Serving Suggestions

Perfect alongside soups, stews, roasts, or as burger buns. Store in airtight container for up to 3 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I freeze these rolls?

Yes, freeze unbaked rolls after shaping. Thaw and rise before baking.
